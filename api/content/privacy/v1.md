## 1. What We Collect

- Account information: name, email, phone number, business details you
  provide during registration and intake.
- Equipment information: photos, descriptions, and condition data you
  submit for appraisal or consignment.
- Usage data: log-in times, IP addresses, device fingerprints used for
  security and fraud prevention.

## 2. How We Use It

- To operate the appraisal, consignment, and listing services you
  request.
- To contact you about your submissions, account, and listings.
- To detect and prevent fraud and abuse.

## 3. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with:

- Service providers that operate our platform under contract.
- Buyers or counterparties you explicitly authorize.
- Government authorities when legally compelled.

## 4. Your Choices

- Access, update, or export your data from your account profile.
- Request deletion of your account; a 30-day grace period precedes
  permanent removal.
- Manage email and SMS preferences at any time.

## 5. Retention

Operational records are retained while your account is active and for a
reasonable period afterward to satisfy legal and contractual
obligations. See the in-product data-export and deletion controls.
